Me, Fiserv and Fundación Omar Dengo created the first team programming competition for students and professionals of the IT area!

Social responsibility solutions is our main goal, we look to create a yearly competition in which FISERV and its allies provide the space and conditions for IT professionals and students to create a product or solution that responds to a specific social conflict in our country.

This year's challenge is to create an application that could be used by Fundación Omar Dengo in pre-school teaching of technology use.
Teams can be of 4 minimum and 5 max!
Participants will have 36 straight hours of programming to complete the challenge.
Teams will constantly receive functionality winning points depending on different criteria, such as completeness, quality and time.
Awesome prizes will be given to the team that finishes in the top 3 places.
